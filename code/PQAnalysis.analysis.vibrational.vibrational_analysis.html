Calculate wavenumbers, force constants, reduced masses and normal modes.

Parameters:

  • atom_masses (np.ndarray) – Atomic masses in amu.

  • atom_coords (np.ndarray) – Atomic coordinates with shape (n_atoms, 3).

  • hessian (np.ndarray) – Cartesian Hessian with shape (3 * n_atoms, 3 * n_atoms).

  • atom_charges (np.ndarray | None, optional) – Atomic charges used to calculate IR intensities, by default None.

  • unit (str, optional) – Hessian energy unit. Supported values are kcal, hartree and ev, by default kcal.

  • hessian_sign (str | float, optional) – Hessian sign convention. Supported values are auto, positive, negative, 1 and -1, by default auto.
